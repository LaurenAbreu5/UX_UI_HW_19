About Me

User Experience Designer

Welcome to my design portfolio! My name is Lauren Abreu, and I am a UX/UI designer with a background in industrial design. My experience in the field of design has enabled me to develop a wide range of skills, including proficiency in various computer design tools for advertising, editorial and illustration pieces. I am also skilled in 3D modeling and rendering tools, which allow me to create realistic and dynamic designs that truly bring ideas to life. Through my portfolio, you will see a diverse range of projects that showcase my skills in UX/UI design, as well as my ability to work across different design disciplines. My goal is to create designs that are not only visually stunning but also user-friendly, intuitive and engaging. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I look forward to the opportunity to work with you.
